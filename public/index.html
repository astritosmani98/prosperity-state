How to play
  • Each round you earn income (it grows as national Prosperity rises).
  • Secretly choose how much to keep vs contribute to the nation.
  • Contributions raise Prosperity; generous rounds are far more efficient.
  • The single biggest giver each round gets a 20% refund (Top Contributor bonus).
  • Every 3 rounds you vote on policy — votes are weighted by your Influence.
  • Hit 0 Coins and you go bankrupt, out of the final ranking.
  • When Prosperity reaches 100 the game ends instantly — richest survivor wins.
